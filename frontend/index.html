HOW TO PLAY

Beat Dax. Don't get caught.

You can't out-ride Dax anymore — but the leaderboard only sees the GPS file you upload, and a file can be edited.

  1. 1
    Doctor the file. Switch on a tool in THE TOOLKIT and drag its sliders. Watch YOUR UPLOAD time drop.
  2. 2
    Beat the crown. Get your time under Dax's — the gap turns orange when you're ahead.
  3. 3
    Survive the audit. Hit UPLOAD RIDE. Forensic checks scan your file. Cheat too hard and the physics catches you.
